Web Development

Web development combines design and coding to create engaging, functional websites and web applications. It bridges creativity and technology, enabling businesses to connect with their audience online effectively.

Software Development

Software development encompasses the entire lifecycle of building software applications, from concept to maintenance. It involves coding, testing, and deployment, ensuring that programs meet user needs and function reliably.

Data Analytics

Data analytics extracts valuable insights from vast datasets, aiding informed decision-making. Techniques like data mining and statistical analysis uncover patterns, trends, and opportunities, empowering businesses to optimize their strategies.

Cybersecurity

Database Management

Database management involves organizing, securing, and optimizing data storage. It ensures data availability and integrity, supporting efficient retrieval and analysis. Effective management is fundamental for data-driven organizations.

Overview of Kenyatta University online professional courses


KU ICT PROFESSIONAL TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION CENTER (IPTCC) sets to deliver ICT training so as to help improve career and educational opportunities for individuals in our community by targeting those who want to enhance their ICT skills at different levels.
The courses have been tailored to cater for individuals who have different levels of education starting from Primary School level to University level.
