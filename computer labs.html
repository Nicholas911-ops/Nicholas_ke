Feel free and most welcome to visit our student lab centers for your own personal IT expo development

Kenyatta University provides students with readily available computer labs tailored for assignments and online learning in critical disciplines like database management, cybersecurity, data analytics, and programming courses. These well-equipped labs offer students essential resources, fostering an ideal learning environment. Whether for research or hands-on coursework, these facilities are instrumental in enriching the academic journey at Kenyatta University, ensuring students have the tools they need to succeed in their chosen fields of study.
